### **Step 1: Install the Chrome Extension**

Interactively’s Chrome Extension logs all user actions, including clicks, scrolls, and typing, and automatically turns them into editable video snippets and screenshots.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Download from the Chrome Web Store" icon="download" iconType="regular">
    1. Go to the Chrome Web Store and click "Add to Chrome"
    2. Open the extension and log in to your Interactively account
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### **Step 2: Record**

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Configure recording settings" icon="gear">
    1. Select a recording mode: `Interactive` , `Video`, or `Screenshot`
    2. **(Optional)** Adjust the recording area by typing in dimensions or selecting a preset size
    3. **(Optional)** Enable camera/microphone for `Video` recording mode
    4. **(Optional)** Make changes to the page by clicking the “Modify Page” button
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Update your site name and colors" icon="palette">
    For a first change, let's update the name and colors of your docs site.

    1. Open `docs.json` in your editor.
    2. Change the `"name"` field to your project name.
    3. Update the `"colors"` to match your brand.
    4. Save and see your changes instantly at `http://localhost:3000`.

    <Tip>
      Try changing the primary color to see an immediate difference\!
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 3: Go live

<Accordion title="Publish your docs" icon="rocket">
  1. Commit and push your changes.
  2. Your docs will update and be live in moments\!
</Accordion>
