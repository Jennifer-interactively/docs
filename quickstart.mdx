## Step 1: Install the Chrome Extension

Interactively's Chrome Extension logs all user actions, including clicks, scrolls, and typing, and automatically turns them into editable video snippets and screenshots.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Download extension from Chrome Web Store" icon="chrome" iconType="regular">
    1. Go to the Chrome Web Store and click "Add to Chrome"
    2. Open the extension and log in to your Interactively account
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Step 2: Record

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Configure recording settings" icon="gear">
    1. Select a recording mode: `Interactive` , `Video`, or `Screenshot`
    2. _(Optional)_ Adjust the recording area by typing in dimensions or selecting a preset size
    3. _(Optional)_ Enable camera/microphone for `Video` recording mode
    4. _(Optional)_ Make changes to the page by pressing `Modify Page`
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Record" icon="video">
    1. Go to the URL you want to record
    2. Press `Record`
    3. Navigate through your product
    4. Click the extension icon to end the recording
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Step 3: Edit

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Edit " icon="pencil">
    1. Select the Edit tab to enter `Edit` mode
    2. Build and customize your demo with our drag-and-drop editor
    3. Add your brand colors and font, and include your logo and website URL in the watermark
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Step 4: Share

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Website embed" icon="code">
    1. Press `Copy code` to copy the embed code
    2. Paste the embed code on the webpage where you want the demo to appear
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Share via link" icon="link">
    1. Press `Copy link` to copy the share link
    2. Share the link anywhere

  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Export as video/GIF" icon="play">
    **Export video:**

    1. _(Optional)_ In **Advanced settings**, configure the `File type`, `Resolution`, and enable or disable `Cursor animation`
    2. Press `Export as video` to download your demo as an .mp4 or .webm file

    **Export GIF:**

    1. _(Optional)_ In **Advanced Settings**, configure the `Clip duration`, `Resolution`, and enable or disable `Cursor animation`.
    2. Press `Export as GIF` to download your demo as a .gif file
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Step 5: Analyze

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Analytics" icon="chart-line">
    1. Navigate to the **Analytics** dashboard 
    2. Filter data by a time period or enter a custom date range
    3. Export analytics data by pressing `Download CSV`
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Demo-level analytics" icon="arrow-trend-up">
    1. Open a demo and select the `Analyze` tab
    2. Filter data by a time period or enter a custom date range
    3. Export analytics data by pressing `Download CSV`
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
